Introduction

This website integrates our four interactive maps that visualize the density of restaurants and hotspot areas with appropriate scale. Just by browsing this site, visitors can gradually understand the content and themes of our group project. Besides, through the intuitive comparison of the static maps, tourists can find the correlations and differences between the four cities chosen.

Compare the 4 cities

Aims

In this project, we chose four well-known touristic cities, which are Barcelona, Edinburgh, London and Las Vegas, to explore how the catering industry describes the life of the cities.
